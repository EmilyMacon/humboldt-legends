Responsible cannabis from the heart of Humboldt

Our Mission

Give the plant back to the people, grow for good, and use business to inspire environmental responsibility.

Humboldt Legends is a group of heritage cannabis cultivators with the highest environmental standards in the industry.

Since 1974, we’ve been perfecting our sustainable methodologies—inspired by our love & respect for the Humboldt County terroir.

Our plants are grown by innovative and sustainable methods — powered by the sun, fed by rain water, and harvested by hand.

Our Values

Artisimal Quality

Our Farmers have decades of experience growing cannabis

Responsibly Grown

Humboldt Legend’s farmers respect the land as well as your health. You won’t find any non-organic chemicals or watershed damaging methods in our 100% sun powered fields.

Unmatched Cannabis Terrior

Humboldt’s unique terroir serves as a foundation for our master farmers to grow and produce the highest quality cannabis.

A Proud B-Corporation

As a proud B-Corp, we give back to the land that has taught and given us so much by continually committing ourselves to protecting it, every chance we get.
